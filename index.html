How to Play

  • Match to Clear

    Tap on three identical 3D objects to collect them. Clear all target objects to advance to the next level.

  • Strategize Your Moves

    Analyze the board layout before making your matches to maximize efficiency.

  • Leverage Boosters

    Use hammers, time extenders, and magic erasers to overcome difficult stages.

  • Participate in Events

    Join regularly scheduled events to earn rare rewards and items that make your journey easier.

  • Offline Play

    No internet? No problem! Enjoy uninterrupted gameplay wherever you are.

  • Focus on collecting the hardest-to-reach objects first.
  • Save your boosters for levels where they're most needed.
  • Don't rush—planning your moves increases your chances of success.

FAQ

  • What is the objective of Toy Room?
    Your goal is to match three identical 3D objects on the board and clear the target objects specified for each level.
  • How do I use boosters?
    Boosters like hammers and erasers can be activated from the in-game menu during a level. Tap on the booster, then select where you want to use it.
  • Can I play Toy Room offline?
    Yes, Toy Room supports offline gameplay. You can enjoy the game anytime without an internet connection.
  • What makes Toy Room different from other match games?
    Toy Room combines beautiful 3D graphics, intuitive controls, ad-free gameplay, and challenging levels, offering a unique and immersive experience.
  • How do I join events?
    Events are automatically available in the game during special occasions. Simply navigate to the event tab from the main menu to participate.
  • Is this game suitable for kids?
    Absolutely! The intuitive controls and colorful design make it ideal for players of all ages.
  • What rewards can I earn in events?
    Event rewards include coins, boosters, and exclusive items that can help you progress faster through the game.
  • What if I get stuck on a level?
    Use boosters to overcome difficult levels, or replay previous levels to practice and refine your strategy.
